16 approaches, zero shader. Each heart is built with plain SVG — no WebGL, no ray-march, no glass. The "3D feel" in some (glossy, radial gradient, low poly) comes purely from SVG gradients, patterns, and triangulation. This keeps it cross-browser stable, trivially animatable, and renders cleanly in any size.
