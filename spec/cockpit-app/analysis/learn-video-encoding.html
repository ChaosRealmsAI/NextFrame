Section 01

一帧是什么（What is a Frame）

视频就是快速播放的一连串静止图片。每张图片就是一帧——一个由彩色像素组成的网格。

1920 x 1080 = 2,073,600 像素
x 3 字节（R, G, B）= ~6 MB 每帧
x 30 fps = 180 MB/s
x 60 秒 = 10.8 GB 每分钟

Section 02

I 帧、P 帧、B 帧——帧类型（Frame Types）

不是每一帧都需要存完整画面。编码器利用时间冗余——帧与帧之间大部分画面是不变的。

I-Frame（关键帧）

完整画面。可以独立解码。类似一张 JPEG 快照。

P-Frame（预测帧）

只存储与前一帧的差异。比 I-frame 小约 70%。

B-Frame（双向预测帧）

同时参考前后帧。体积最小。比 I-frame 小约 85%。

GOP——图片组（Group of Pictures）

帧按重复模式组织成 GOP。一个典型的模式：

I-Frame（关键帧） P-Frame（预测帧） B-Frame（双向预测帧）
NextFrame 把 HTML 录制为截图——每一帧都是 I-frame。编码器（VideoToolbox / x264）必须分析它们并创建 P/B 帧来压缩。
Section 03

编解码器 vs 容器（Codec vs Container）

Codec = 语言

如何压缩和解压视频数据。把原始像素转换成紧凑比特流的算法。

例：H.264, H.265, VP9, AV1

Container = 信封

如何把视频 + 音频 + 元数据打包到一个文件里。只是一个封装格式。

例：.mp4, .webm, .mov, .mkv

Codec Container Quality Speed Compat
H.264 .mp4 Good Fast Universal
H.265 / HEVC .mp4 Better Slower Most
VP9 .webm Good Slow Web
AV1 .mp4 / .webm Best Very slow New only
ProRes .mov Near-lossless Fast Apple/Pro
NextFrame uses H.264 in .mp4 — best compatibility across all platforms and players.

Section 05

码率控制 — Bitrate Control

Three ways to tell the encoder how many bits to spend:

CBR

固定码率 — Same bitrate throughout. Good for streaming, wastes bits on simple scenes.

VBR

可变码率 — More bits for complex scenes, fewer for simple. Better quality per byte.

CRF

质量目标 — Encoder decides bitrate. Best for offline rendering. What NextFrame uses.

Bitrate Over Time

CBR wastes bits on simple scenes (horizontal line stays high). VBR and CRF allocate more bits where complexity demands it — the spike in the middle is a complex scene.

CRF lets the encoder be smart about bitrate allocation. For offline rendering (not streaming), it produces the best quality-per-byte.

The Gotcha

Audio must be handled separately. You cannot split a continuous audio stream into chunks the same way you split frame rendering.

Why Frame-Pure Matters

Each WebView can jump to any frame independently. React/state-based renderers CAN'T do this — they need sequential state updates.

Parallel recording is only possible because NextFrame's timeline is frame-pure — any frame can be rendered without knowing any other frame's state.
Section 09

音视频同步 — Audio-Video Sync

Audio is a continuous waveform. Video is discrete frames. Keeping them in sync requires precise timestamps.

The Problem

Video
Audio
PTS
0.0s0.5s1.0s1.5s2.0s2.5s3.0s

PTS (Presentation Timestamp)

Every frame and audio sample has a timestamp: "show me at exactly 3.000 seconds." If frame 90 at 30fps should appear at 3.0s but appears at 3.03s — you see lip-sync drift.

NextFrame's Approach

TTS generates audio with word-level timestamps. Video frames are calculated from the same timestamps. Same source of truth = guaranteed sync.

Audio and video sync through shared timestamps. NextFrame derives both audio timing and frame timing from the same TTS word timestamps — sync is structural, not corrective.
